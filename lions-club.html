About the Lions Club

The North Webster Lions Club is a dedicated group of community volunteers committed to making a difference in the lives of those around them. As part of Lions Clubs International — the world's largest service organization — our local club carries forward a proud tradition of service.

Since 1946, the Lions Club has been the heart behind the Mermaid Festival, organizing and running the event each year to bring the community together and raise funds for important local causes.

Every dollar raised through the festival goes right back into supporting the North Webster community through scholarships, vision care, community improvements, and more.

Community Impact

The Mermaid Festival directly supports these community initiatives and more.

Scholarships

Providing educational scholarships to local students pursuing higher education.

Vision Care

Supporting vision screenings, eyeglasses, and eye care for those in need — a core Lions Club mission.

Community Projects

Funding parks, facilities, and infrastructure improvements that benefit every resident of North Webster.

Youth Programs

Supporting youth activities, sports, and development programs throughout the community.
