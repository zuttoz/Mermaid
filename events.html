Dinosaur Takeover

Dinosaur Takeover

An interactive dinosaur experience featuring up to 8 life-sized dinosaurs, including a 50-foot brachiosaurus. Watch a baby dinosaur hatch, see dinosaurs get "trained," and kids can play hide-and-seek with a 12-foot velociraptor. Daily free shows and exhibits. Learn more

Free
Bike Giveaways

Bike Giveaways

One girl's bike and one boy's bike given away! Free tickets are handed out after the Cutie Parade at the festival grounds office. Winners are drawn at 8 p.m. at the stage area.

Free
Carnival Rides

Carnival Rides

A traveling carnival midway with rides for kids and adults. Buy individual ride tickets or grab a wristband for unlimited rides all day.

Paid
Coin Hunt

Lake City Bank Coin Hunt

Proudly sponsored by Lake City Bank. $50 in coins scattered in the sand lot by the stage for each age group (ages 3-5 and 6-9). Kids keep whatever they find! Quarters, dimes, and more hidden throughout.

Free
Cutie Parade

Cutie Parade

  • Open to children under 14
  • Three categories: Walking, Hand-Drawn, or Motorized units
  • No entry fee
  • Line-up/check-in at 5:00 p.m. at North Webster Community Church, must be checked in by 6:15 p.m.
  • Parade begins at 6:30 p.m.
  • Educational bonds up to $5,000 per category (Shoop Sports and Youth Foundation)
  • Application deadline applies, no late registrations accepted

Download application for full rules

Free
Cutie Pageant

Cutie King & Queen Pageant

  • Open to children ages 5-7
  • Held at North Webster Community Center
  • Registration 6:15 - 6:45 p.m., no late check-in allowed
  • Pageant begins promptly at 7:00 p.m.
  • Educational bonds up to $5,000 (Shoop Sports and Youth Foundation)
  • Must participate in Cutie Parade to be eligible
  • Application deadline applies, no late registrations accepted

Download application for full rules

Free
Demolition Derby

Demolition Derby

Drivers compete in vehicle demolition heats on the festival grounds. Saturday at 7:00 p.m. Free for spectators, drivers pay an entry fee per car.

Free to Watch
Festival Parade

Festival Parade

The main festival parade through downtown North Webster on Main Street starting Saturday at 4:00 p.m. Features floats, marching bands, and community groups celebrating this year's theme.

Free
Strawberry Festival

Strawberry Festival

Hosted by North Webster United Methodist Church on the festival grounds. Strawberry ice cream, pies, shortcake, and more. 4:00 - 7:30 p.m.

Paid
Festival Food

Food

Wawasee Band pork burgers at the Lions Club food stand, Kiwanis ice cream, plus a variety of local food vendors. Vendors change each year so there's always something new to try!

Paid
Karaoke

Karaoke

Take the stage and show off your singing skills! Karaoke entertainment on the festival stage. Saturday starting at 6:00 p.m.

Free
Laser Tag

Laser Tag

Tactical laser tag set up on the festival grounds. Fun for kids and adults.

Free
Local Bands

Local Bands

Live music performances on Merlin's Stage throughout the festival. Local and regional acts perform evening shows. Check the schedule above for days, times, and bands.

Free
Power Wheels Race

Power Wheels Race

Open to children up to 8 years old. Power Wheels are provided, but participants may bring their own if desired. Saturday, sign-up at 11:30 a.m., race starts at noon. Each age group winner receives a prize, and all participants receive trophies.

Free
Queen of Lakes Pageant

Queen of Lakes Pageant

A scholarship pageant held at the North Webster Community Center. Saturday at 7:00 p.m. Young women compete for the Queen of the Lakes title and scholarship money funded by the Shoop Sports and Youth Foundation.

Hot Air Balloon Ride

RE/MAX Hot Air Balloon Ride

Take a tethered hot air balloon ride on the festival grounds, courtesy of RE/MAX. Weather permitting.

Free
Tournament of Champions

Tournament of Champions

The Shoop Sports and Youth Foundation Tournament of Champions at North Webster Elementary School. A ten-event athletic skills competition where area youth athletes compete for college scholarship money.
