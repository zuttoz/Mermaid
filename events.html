Mermaid Queen Pageant

One of the festival's most cherished traditions, the Mermaid Queen Pageant crowns the Queen of Lakes and her court. Contestants showcase their talents, poise, and community involvement in this celebrated event.

Event Photo

Grand Parade

The highlight of the festival! Watch floats, marching bands, community groups, and more wind through the streets of North Webster in our spectacular annual parade.

Cutie King & Queen Contest

Our youngest community members take the spotlight! Watch adorable little ones compete for the title of Cutie King and Queen in this heartwarming event the whole family will love.

Live Entertainment

Carnival Rides & Games

Fireworks Display
